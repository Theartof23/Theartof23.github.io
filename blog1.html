Dfo Family Reunion

James Griffin
06.11.18

DFO or Degenerat flame off is an event that has been going on in Eugene, for years, but after a year off moved to Porland for this years event. The even took place in Gresham Oregon and was a 2 day event this year housing some of the best glass artists in the world. The first day was filled with demonstrations where two artists would collaborate on a piece, whereas the second day was individual artsists creating their own works.

Photos above show some cold work engraving being done by Steve Bishop and a collaboration piece made by Robert Mickelson and Liquid Glass Arts.
